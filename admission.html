Teaching Methodology

We follow semester scheme for all the 4 years of UG courses and 2 years of PG courses. Teaching Methodology involves Three Tier learning process. Theoretical knowledge is provided through classroom teaching using chalk-duster to modern teaching aids. Online lectures are also explored through NPTEL, QEEE under Technical Education Quality Improvement Program. Students are facilitated for problem solving and doubt clearing in the subjects specified in the schemes of syllabi. Practical sessions are the part of curriculum prescribed by Rajasthan Technical University (RTU), Kota exploring the theoretical knowledge over practice. Industrial visits are also arranged for better exposure to latest technological advancements.
